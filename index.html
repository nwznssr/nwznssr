LeakSafe - Your Ultimate Leak Detection Solution

About LeakSafe

LeakSafe is a smart and portable gadget designed to detect leaks under household sinks and alert you instantly. This self-made gadget uses a microbit, tinfoil strips, a water collection dish, and a portable base to sense any leaks and send a radio signal to other microbits located in different rooms of your house. It's an easy, affordable, and efficient way to prevent water damage!

Components

  • Microbit: Acts as the main controller and sends signals when a leak is detected.
  • Tinfoil Strips: Detects water by completing a circuit when wet.
  • Water Collection Dish: Catches small leaks and activates the alert system.
  • Portable Base: Allows for easy placement under sinks.

How it Works

Simply place LeakSafe under your sink, and it will monitor the area for any signs of water. When water touches the tinfoil strips, a signal is sent to the microbit, which immediately broadcasts a radio alert to other microbits around your house. You can place multiple microbits in different rooms to stay informed no matter where you are.
